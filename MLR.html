Estimating Weight of the fish

This Machine learning model used Multiple Linear Regression to estimate the Weight of the fish. It uses the weight as the dependent variable while the other variables are used as the independent variable and model the relationship between the dependent variable and the independent variables.

Photo by Jakub Kapusnak on Unsplash

Background


This dataset consist of the 7 most common fish species in the fish market sales.

  • Species - Species of fish
  • Weight - Weight of fish in gram
  • Length1 - Vertical length of fish in cm
  • Length2 - Diagonal length of fish in cm
  • Length3 - Cross length of fish in cm
  • Height - Height in cm
  • Width - Diagonal width in cm
