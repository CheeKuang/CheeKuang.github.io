Customer Segmentation

This model uses PCA and K mean clustering to segment the customers into different clusters. The use of PCA helps reduce the number of features, improving the algorithm's performance. By clustering the customers into different segments, it will help to understand the customer cluster better and maximize the value of the customer group as other clusters have different ways of approaching them.

Photo by Ryoji Iwata on Unsplash

Background


This dataset consists of 2000 individuals from a store. The details were collected via their loyalty card. There are 8 columns in the dataset

  • ID - individual Id
  • Sex - 0(Male) 1 (Female)
  • Marital Status - 0(single) 1 (not single)
  • Age - Age of individual
  • Education - 0(unknown) 1 (high school) 2(university) 3(Graduate School)
  • Income - Annual income in dollars
  • Occupation - 0(unemployed) 1(Skilled) 2(highly qualified)
  • Settlement Size - 0(small) 1(mid sized) 2(big) cities
